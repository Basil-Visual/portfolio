About

More than correction. This is image design.

I’m Basil, a DaVinci Resolve colorist. I treat color like part of the story. Sometimes that means a bold cinematic mood. Sometimes it means keeping the image clean, natural, and consistent from shot to shot.

Signature

Controlled contrast

Protect highlights, keep blacks rich, and preserve texture.

Skin tone respect

Natural hue and believable saturation, even under mixed light.
